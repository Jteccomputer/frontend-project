Brief History

Homes Alive Academy was formed in the year 2017 at Jesus Avenue, Apo, Abuja. It was formed with robust curriculum and very high standards in view,
encompassing British/Nigerian Curriculum, structured with Montessori way. Our Teachers are graduates and highly experienced,
irrespective of its environment.

Montessori Education is a much higher education than the regular education, as it uses more of objects,
audio and visual effects to pass down knowledge to children.

There are about five more subjects under the Montessori Education, that Homes Alive Academy does which are not taught in regular schools.
